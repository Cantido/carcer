King [[Trinkerly Vitrex von Sufto]]'s advisor. A gaunt, grey-haired man. He knows the inner workings of the city and oversees important events if the King or [[Aurora Camilieri Ethiran von Sufto|Queen]] cannot attend directly.

[[Adelheid Theophanu Gisela von Sufto|Ada]] has interacted with him plenty before, and has heard him and the king discuss a "drain" on society and the economy, and how each year they find more and more people they need to reach out to to ensure they don't become a drain on society.

It turns out the solution to the "drain" is to massacre the sick and homeless, as they expected us to do during [[Session 20]].
