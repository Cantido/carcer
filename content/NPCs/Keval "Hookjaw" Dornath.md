A towering individual with a wiry frame and a permanent snarl, Keval is one of [[The Undervein]]’s most feared enforcers. His nickname comes from the jagged, steel hook replacing his lower jaw—a gruesome injury he wears as a badge of honor. Keval oversees smuggling operations through the hidden ports along [[Locations/Carcer/Gororak/Hiro Archipelago/Suftois/index|Suftois]]'s coastline, ensuring shipments of Vourson Mist, exotic weapons, and rare artifacts flow without interruption.
