The northern and largest land mass of [[Locations/Carcer/Gororak/index|Gororak]]. It is largely jungle-covered, forested, and humid. The eastern half of the continent is named the [[Gosodyani Wilds]]. In the central western lands, the Pel-A-Dar Peaks run from the north and south sides of the continent.

Most of the land is covered by the Gosodyani Wilds, dangerous and deep jungles that house creatures and beings of ancient times. Most travel is done around the coast because of this.
