An island of [[Locations/Carcer/Gororak/index|Gororak]].

A democratic space whose leader, [[Spring Bloom of the Sunned Sands Clan]], strives to make it a safe place of virtue and serenity. Spring Bloom welcomes all, though it means the island's resources are stretched thin.
