title: Hiro Archipelago
---
A cluster of islands south of [[Locations/Carcer/Gororak/Grissau/index|Grissau]]. Four large islands make up the bulk of the archipelago. There are also three small islands between the four large ones, called [[Locations/Carcer/Gororak/Hiro Archipelago/The Triplets/index|The Triplets]].
