Known as Ada to those close to her.

## Appearance
Ada is graceful and beautiful. Wavy, reddish-brown hair frames her face, and is usually kept in a tidy bun while she is working. Her skin is the color of porcelain, and her eyes are a warm brown.

## Personality
Despite her soft appearance, Ada is loud, coarse, and insensitive. She lives for her vices: smoking, drinking, and gambling. She frequently gets into bar-fights and has challenged many men to duels, which she rarely loses. Even the shadier characters that visit her at the bar genuinely enjoy her company, including those whose asses she’s had to kick once or twice.

## Backstory
What she keeps to herself is that she is the daughter of Queen [[Aurora Camilieri Ethiran von Sufto]] & King [[Trinkerly Vitrex von Sufto]] of [[Locations/Carcer/Gororak/Hiro Archipelago/Suftois/index|Suftois]], and she was exiled from their kingdom for treason against the crown. Her full name is Adelheid Theophanu Gisela von Sufto, and her treason was gambling away a piece of land that then fell into the hands of the rival warlord [[Mistress Gratenova Flame Kissed]].

## Goals
She wants revenge. She knows the entire royal court is corrupt to its core, and she’s spiteful that they would dare to exile her while committing far more heinous crimes in secret.

## Relationships
### King [[Trinkerly Vitrex von Sufto]]
Ada was rarely able to speak to her father, let alone spend any quality time with him. She received more written communication from him than spoken, and she felt like just another subject of his kingdom.

### Queen [[Aurora Camilieri Ethiran von Sufto]]
At the very least, Ada felt slightly more affection from her mother than from her father. They were able to speak together, albeit infrequently.

### [[Sophia Magdeburg]]
Ada formed a very close (even romantic) bond with her lady-in-waiting, Sophia. Sophia is the one person Ada felt she could be vulnerable around in an otherwise cutthroat political landscape.
### [[Sithan Kere‘auna]]
At the age of 18, Ada was arranged to be married to Sithan Kere’auna. Even if she had met him, her heart was with another, and so she refused, leaving immediately at the suggestion of the idea. This led her to make some of the life changing choices that she lives with to this day.
