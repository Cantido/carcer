[[Thorn Slakter|Thorn]] collapses. He feels exhausted, his chest heavy. The visions he saw indicated that [[Veltran's Deep]] is near the surface.

The Veilspine Relic is now a white spiral on Thorn's hand. Now, whenever he places his hand upon the obelisk, his visions direct him along the beam of light it projects, going past Veltran's Deep, towards something much farther away, and much deeper: A labyrinthine structure of coral.

We wish to track down Veltran's Deep, as well as [[Queen Alendria|The Drowned Queen]], but first we need to make some money. So, we board our ship and make our way back.

We do some fishing off the side of the ship, and catch quite a bit of fish on our first week of travel. Less so the second week.

With one week left to [[Locations/Carcer/Gororak/Hiro Archipelago/Nesuvian Cay/Port Soruka/index|Port Soruka]], we decide to make a stop in [[Locations/Carcer/Gororak/Hiro Archipelago/Nesuvian Cay/Port Navira/index|Port Navira]] and look for passengers we could potentially transport and make some money from.
- [[Tula Tuga|Tula]] looks for moderately wealthy passengers, but doesn't do too well. She finds a poor mother & child looking for passage
- [[Aster]] looks for grizzled folk who don't mine our sparse accommodations, and who have cargo. He finds one guy who meets the description. The man is headed for [[Repenson Refuge]] but wants to stop in Port Soruka anyway. We can take him the full way or just to Soruka, he doesn't mind.
- [[Saragin Zelgath|Gin]] is looking for someone wealthy. He finds an insufferable jerk who we believe will tip us well if give him a good experience.

Meanwhile, [[Rhask Ocomzu|Rhask]] takes the forged manifest we found in [[Session 1]] to [[The Warden]], hoping that it would point us in [[Captain Harlow]]'s direction. The Warden tells us that, since we cut off the supply of Vourson Mist to [[Locations/Carcer/Gororak/Hiro Archipelago/Nesuvian Cay/index|Nesuvian Cay]], Harlow is making deals further south.

Aster and [[Adelheid Theophanu Gisela von Sufto|Ada]] find the fanciest bar in town (the Salted Net), and ask to see their most expensive bottle. After getting a good look at the 15,000 gp bottle, we politely decline to try it and purchase a much more affordable bottle for 30 gp. Ada then forges a new label for this bottle to look like the expensive bottle's label. We plan on serving this to the wealthy passenger and scamming him.

After purchasing this bottle, we now have 355 gp.

We also improve our furnishings in the captain's quarters of our ship, spending 25 gp.

Finally, we load everyone up and head to [[Locations/Carcer/Gororak/Hiro Archipelago/Nesuvian Cay/Port Soruka/index|Port Soruka]].

We do a variety of things to entertain our wealthy guest. On the last day of the journey, Ada serves the forged liquor to him, and offer him the bottle for 7,500 gp. He's feeling generous, and gives us 10,000 gp for it, along with an extra 1,600 gp for the trip. The mother gives us 6 gp, the merchant gives us 180 gp, and we earn 450 gp from fishing.

We now have 12,566 gp in party funds, but we have some payments to make:
- 480 gp (30%) of our passenger fees go to the crew
- 375 gp to pay off the remainder of our house debt
- 745 gp we leave with [[Spring Bloom of the Sunned Sands Clan]] to take care of repairs to the house

That leaves 10,964 gp of party funds.

We then go to [[Lande]] for pressure pearls, and tell him we are investigating [[Veltran's Deep]]. He immediately change tone. He hasn't been there, but it's the homeland of the Triton race and outsiders are not supposed to know about it.

Lande tells us we will not need pressure pearls to access Veltran's Deep. However, we will likely need his 500 gp pressure pearls to reach wherever the Drowned Queen might be.

We decide to buy eight 100-gp (100 fathom) pressure pearls from Lande.

Lande warns us to be on our best behavior, because even in the best case scenario, as outsiders we will be arrested and brought to the authorities.

We ask Spring Bloom to coordinate repairs of the house so [[Sophia Magdeburg]] can come with us. Spring Bloom asks if some of the money can go towards fixing up other houses in the neighborhood, and we allow it.
