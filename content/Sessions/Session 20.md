We find a blacksmith to upgrade our armor.

We then gathered offerings to [[Mahina Kai]] like shells & sea glass, and then we make offerings at the [[Temple of Mahina Kai]].

That night, we have a big fight at the [[Azure Amphitheater]]. Once in the theater, we were presented with a large mob of sick, homeless, and elderly people, as well as orphan children, and we are ordered to kill them all. We refuse, and [[Dastrone Lecktree]] summons a corpse monster to kill us all.

We defeat the corpse monster, and save a small number of the civilians, though the monster killed many of them.
