We are at [[Wavetide Hollow]], and decide to search for the artifact that we learned about from [[Velmira Sorrel]]'s notes when we landed.

After traveling south a while, [[Tula Tuga|Tula]] spots a cavern in the rocks on the shore, and we head in to explore. Inside, we find writing in Deep Speech along with murals depicting a large city at the bottom of the ocean, and a large depiction of a woman. Some carvings in this cavern look fresh, and they're in a different language.

We soon find [[Velmira Sorrel]] writing on the walls and drawing eyes with chalk. She refuses to cooperate with us, and among the nonsense she repeats "thirty feet" and "five fathoms."

[[Saria Nylora]] prepared Comprehend Language, and points out a poem. It's a riddle about freeing someone by collecting [[The Five Fathoms]].

[[Aster]] dives into the deeper water in the cavern, and finds the bottom. It is littered with bodies. Among the bodies he finds a bright white ivory coral carving of a spiral. He scoops it up with a piece of clothing, but as he does, a rush of bubbles holds him to the bottom. He messages [[Rhask Ocomzu|Rhask]] who jumps in to help, but is pulled down as well.

With an incredible Athletics check and a Misty Step, Aster manages to escape the water. Once he is out, the bubbles stop, and Rhask can swim to the surface.

Velmira sees the thing Aster brought up, and says "four fathoms," and restates the poem about the challenge. She climbs into the water, and black tendrils of mist appear. They turn her to glass, and drag her into the depths. We run and narrowly escape to dry land.

We decide to head back to our ship to regroup.

Once on the ship, [[Thorn Slakter|Thorn]] is unresponsive. He can walk around, but is just staring off into space. We help him back into the captain's quarters. With a cast of Detect Magic, we see Thorn enveloped in black mist. The crew tells us that he got quiet around sundown.

We move the ship away from shore.

Before bed, [[LaRea Yilda|LaRea]] contacts [[The Twins]] and he confirms that he ate our runaway crew member.

We take a long rest. 

[[Sissara]] casts comprehend languages and reads the obelisk on the beach. It contains the second poem on the [[The Five Fathoms]] page.

We place our new coral relic on top of the broken obelisk, and it sends off a bright beam of light. At the same time, a black mist creeps up the shore. We quickly knock it off, and the mist recedes.

This relic is called "The Veilspine Relic."
