> [!info]
>  We were missing four players this sesson, only Rosa, Kat, and Nick were present with Alex, so this session was just a few ocean encounters

We search for survivors of the fight between [[The Twins]] and The Wavecutter, and find one. Thorn pulls him into the deep. We also pull arrows out of corpses, in order to obscure the fact that we turned on them.

[[Adelheid Theophanu Gisela von Sufto|Ada]] forges a new manifest (and rolls a 7), as well as a new license for this ship (and rolls a 16). The new license gives our ship the name "The Stormkeeper."

We sail for [[Locations/Carcer/Gororak/Hiro Archipelago/Nesuvian Cay/Port Soruka/index|Port Soruka]] and had three encounters.

## First encounter
One of our crew goes missing. He took a rowboat but no supplies. Given we were in the middle of the ocean, this seems unwise. The missing crewman's name was [[Viebros]]. We use Sending to message [[The Twins]] about it, because we suspect he may tell [[Trinkerly Vitrex von Sufto|the King of Suftois]] about our survival.

## Second encounter
We see a small island in the distance. Barely a sandbar in the middle of the ocean. On it, there is a table surrounded by chairs, set up for a feast. Three of us ([[Adelheid Theophanu Gisela von Sufto|Ada]], [[LaRea Yilda|LaRea]], and [[Thorn Slakter|Thorn]]) row a ship over to investigate after much deliberation.

Ada grabs a drink, takes a swig, and immediately collapses, unconscious. Thorn takes a bite of food, and also collapses. LaRea pulls three rations out of her pack and places them on the table, then takes a sip of a drink and _does not collapse_.

LaRea sits down at the table, and silhouettes appear in the other chairs, along with a Triton girl. She introduces herself as [[Veridian]]. She tells us that she did not like Ada & Thorn stealing her food, and appreciates that LaRea left something in reciprocity. After LaRea searches Thorn & Ada's pockets for things she can offer to Veridian, they wake up. We sit down at the table and see LaRea and Veridian.

Veridian likes LaRea and gives her a gift, "The Gauntlets of Ogre Power," and asks us to "let her friends know she says hi." Apparently we will know when we meet them.

## Third encounter
Pieces of the ship begin disappearing. During the many days of our journey back to Port Soruka, we continuously investigate, but find no clues. The crew knows nothing. We place a watch, and boards disappear from in front of them in the blink of an eye. Pieces of the sail disappear, seemingly cut.

We decide to "re-keel" the ship, because it is supposedly bad luck to re-name a ship, which we just did. So, once we arrive in Port Soruka, we buy ritual materials like incense, as well as a bottle of sparkling wine, and re-keel the ship under its new name.
