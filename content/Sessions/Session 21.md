We are presented to the [[Trinkerly Vitrex von Sufto|King]] and [[Aurora Camilieri Ethiran von Sufto|Queen]]. The king accuses us of cowardice, not making the "right" choice when it is difficult. He is aware of our plot to get the Wavecutter back up and running to fight [[The Twins]], and says to either return with their heads or not at all.

[[Asmara]] wants us to have some trusted individuals row back to Suftois to inform the king that the dragons were killed and the Wavecutter sunk and we all died.
