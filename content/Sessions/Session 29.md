We use Sending to contact [[Sissara]] and set up a meeting at the [[Sultry Shell]]. While we wait for the meeting, we check the bounty board, but there is nothing of note.

We go to the [[Sentinel Lighthouse]] and speak to [[Spring Bloom of the Sunned Sands Clan|Spring Bloom]] about buying [[The Old Mayor's House]]. She offers it for 750 gold, and we will pay it in two payments of 375 gold. We will renovate it.

[[Adelheid Theophanu Gisela von Sufto|Ada]] asks [[Sophia Magdeburg|Sophia]] to handle the renovation.

Once we meet with Sissara, we ask her to join us on the trip to Wavetide Hollow.

We then hop on our ship and head to [[Wavetide Hollow]]. It takes us four weeks.

The place is a faint memory of what it was. We notice there are no birds in the area, and we hear a faint whispering. There's a "thrum" that draws us in.

[[Rhask Ocomzu|Rhask]] can make out some signs of disturbance on the beach, and we drag our boats further in to hide them.

As we walk towards some pillars, Rhask is hit with a crossbow bolt. We take cover, no one shows themselves, and no more shots come. After we wait a bit, and nothing else happens, we relax.

The air faintly shines under Detect Magic.

We rotate the base of a broken obelisk, and it glows in the shape of the full obelisk.

A kobold jumps out from the rubble and tells us to stop. His name is [[Kozzin Shimmerflick]]. He mentions [[Velmira Sorrel|The Professor]] who is the only other person here, but "her mind broke." He has some of her journals.
